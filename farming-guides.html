Introduction

Organic farming is a method of crop and livestock production that involves much more than choosing not to use pesticides, fertilizers, genetically modified organisms, antibiotics, and growth hormones.

This comprehensive guide will walk you through the fundamental principles and practices of organic farming, helping you understand how to transition to and maintain a successful organic farming operation.
